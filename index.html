What Is Jwebi

Created in 2014 and based in Paris, Jwebi is a collaborative platform which offers a package transport service between individuals in France and abroad. It thus connects travelers who have room in their suitcases or in the trunk of their car, with individuals who are looking to send a parcel in France or abroad, or to buy a product abroad. Jwebi is thus a parcel-sharing platform for parcels nicknamed "blablacar parcel" by the press. But that's not all, Jwebi now allows its users to also use professional carriers for any large package shipment, or even too large to be supported by an individual. These are handpicked professionals who offer the best value for money. A package to send urgently? Quickly contact a traveler or driver on Jwebi, and your package will arrive quickly and inexpensively. Do you want to buy this novelty not found at home? Log on to Jwebi, and select the traveler who can buy it and bring it back for you. Are you moving and looking for the best professional transporter to help you? Consult the profiles of our professionals on Jwebi. Are you going on a weekend and would like to amortize the cost of your trip? Record your journey on Jwebi and accommodate one or more jwebers. Thanks to package carpooling, everyone wins: the sender sends his package inexpensively, the traveler provides service while making the most of his travel costs and the professional transporter optimizes the loading of his vehicles. Among the professional carriers, we have selected only those. The icing on the cake, all shipments transiting via Jwebi are insured up to € 2,000 by MAIF against breakage, theft or any accidental damage occurring during the transport of the package. And this, at no additional cost.
